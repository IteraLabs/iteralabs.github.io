We develop and maintain the following opensource projects.

lucien

The lucid agent to train/optimize and callibrate/fine-tune LLMs

Python wrapper on top of Rust and Cuda engines github::lucien, crates.io::lucien, pypi.org::lucien.

atelier

The workshop where to train and fine-tune the models.

A containerized rust-based framework for High Performant Computing that we, at itera, continously use and develop in order to conduct cognitive research for synthetic intelligence. github::atelier, crates.io::atelier.

deml-research lab

The research group and academic alliance.

A grouping initiative for research purposes, instantiated and held within the collaboration of the Mathematics and Physics Department @ ITESO University. deml-research.github.io

contribute

We aim to be an Opensource non-profit organization only for research purposes towards Opensourced AI. If you are interested, do contribute to the base code for lucien, atelier or opensource academic-research within the deml-research lab.
